## Steps to Update Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Log in at `https://prcs.example.com` and click your name in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Details">
    Click 'Edit Profile', update your information (name, department, contact) as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click 'Save'. Your profile will be updated instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
