## Registering on PRCS

PRCS is available for company employees via email sign-up. Follow these steps:

1. Visit `https://prcs.example.com/register`
2. Enter your company email address
3. Set a password and click "Register"
4. Check your email inbox for a confirmation link
5. Click the link to activate your account

<Callout kind="info">
If you cannot find the confirmation email, check your spam folder or contact Артем Войскович for assistance.
</Callout>
