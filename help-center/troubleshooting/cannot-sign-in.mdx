---

## Symptoms
- Login page shows 'Invalid credentials'
- Password reset emails are not received

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Make sure you're using your company email to sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click 'Forgot password?' and follow the link in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email Артем Войскович at support@prcs.example.com if the problem persists.
  </Step>
</Steps>
