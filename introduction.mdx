## Overview

PRCS provides a secure corporate service platform where you sign in or register using your email address. You access essential employee services, resources, and company tools through a centralized dashboard. Designed for efficiency, PRCS streamlines your daily workflows while maintaining enterprise-grade security.

## Key Benefits for Employees

PRCS empowers you with quick access to services tailored for corporate environments. Benefit from single sign-on, personalized dashboards, and real-time updates on company resources.

## Core Features at a Glance

Explore the main capabilities that make PRCS your go-to platform:

- **Employee Dashboard**: View personalized services, notifications, and quick links.
- **Service Requests**: Submit and track requests for IT support, HR resources, or facilities.
- **Profile Management**: Update your details securely at any time.
- **Integration Ready**: Connects seamlessly with company tools like email and calendars.

<Callout kind="info">
  PRCS supports single sign-on via email. Ensure you use your corporate email address (`your.name@company.com`) for access.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running with PRCS in just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to the PRCS dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In or Register" icon="log-in">
    Enter your corporate email. Follow the prompts to verify and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Services" icon="activity">
    Access your personalized dashboard. Review available services and submit your first request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For programmatic access, use this example to authenticate via API:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/auth/login', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: { 'Content-Type': 'application/json' },
    body: JSON.stringify({ email: 'your.name@company.com' })
  });
  const data = await response.json();
  console.log(data.token);
  ```
  ```python
  import requests
  response = requests.post(
      'https://api.example.com/auth/login',
      json={'email': 'your.name@company.com'}
  )
  data = response.json()
  print(data['token'])
  ```
</CodeGroup>
