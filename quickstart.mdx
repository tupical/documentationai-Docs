## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A valid company email address (e.g., `employee@yourcompany.com`)
- Access to your email inbox for verification
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
</Callout>

No software installation required. PRCS runs entirely in your browser.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register using your email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the PRCS Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** or **Register**. Enter your company email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from PRCS. Click the link inside.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Password" icon="lock">
    Return to the dashboard, enter a secure password, and complete registration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Registration takes under 2 minutes.

## Sign In to PRCS

Once registered, sign in securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page" icon="log-in">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials" icon="key">
    Input your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Authentication" icon="shield">
    If prompted, complete two-factor authentication via email or app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Save your credentials in a secure password manager. Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
</Callout>

## Access Your First Employee Service

Request IT support as your first service.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Search Services">
        Use the search bar: type `IT support`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Submit Request">
        Click **New Request**, fill in details, and submit.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Integration" icon="code">
    After login, use your API token for programmatic access.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
      ````javascript
      const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/services', {
        headers: {
          'Authorization': `Bearer YOUR_TOKEN`
        }
      });
      const services = await response.json();
      console.log(services);
      ````

      ````bash
      curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
           https://api.example.com/v1/services
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Replace `YOUR_TOKEN` with the token from your profile settings.
